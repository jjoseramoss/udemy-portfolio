My Top 3 Movies of all-time

ROCKY

This is my favorite movie series because everytime that I watch the movies I get motivated, and it reminds me of watching movies with my father which I enjoy greatly.

INTERSTELLAR

Another Great Movie Choice, as this movie has INSANE visuals and great action as well as beautiful scenery, This movie greatly inspired me to want to become a space engineer. Hopefully I get to go into that career.

IRON CLAW

This movie is just crazy, the insane amount of plot twists and action as well as drama just all ties in together and the acting is great, I was extremely engaged and it was a great memory as I was watching the movie with my family.
